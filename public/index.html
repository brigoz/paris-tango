Paris Tango

Tango with Brigitte is based in London, UK. Best known for the sumptuous Fire & Flame Ball in London and Chateau tango holidays in France. We organise Argentine tango events, festivals, classes and workshops in the UK, and tango holidays in France. We represent in the UK carefully selected Argentine tango teachers and musicians. We host many internationally renowned dancers and musicians. We offer private tuition for beginners and all levels of dance at our venue in South Hampstead. Private parties and functions: Performances, introduction to tango, first steps and demonstrations. Weddings: Learn to dance for your big day and open the ball in style. We will help you find the most suitable classes and teachers to suit your needs. If you are a visitor we will inform you where to dance in the UK and in France during your visit. We can also help you find suitable accommodation.
